My Aerial Photography & Videography

Welcome to my portfolio of drone photography and videography showcasing the stunning landscapes of Norway's Trøndelag region. As a drone operator based in Stjørdal, I specialize in capturing the natural beauty of central Norway from unique aerial perspectives.

My work focuses on highlighting the dramatic fjords, peaceful lakes, rolling hills, and picturesque communities that make this region so special. Below you'll find a selection of my photographs and videos, all captured using professional drone equipment to ensure the highest quality results.

Note: The images below are compressed for web viewing, and videos have been downsampled to 30fps from 60fps for smoother online performance. High-resolution versions are available upon request for commercial projects.
